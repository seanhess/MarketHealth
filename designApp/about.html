About Us

PriceMyMRI is an easy to use, online service for consumers to find pricing information for medical procedures. Based on National and State averages and user provided data, a visitor can make a decision as to what a reasonable cost is and find service providers within their area. PriceMyMRI is the initial product launch for Market Health LLC, a community based service that provides transparency for medical procedure costs and increased competition within the sector.
